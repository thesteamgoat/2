Hydroelectric Dam

Hydroelectric Power

How It Works

  • Hydroelectric power is electricity made from the energy of moving water in a dam.
  • Water flows through the dam blocking the flow of water and hits the blades of turbines.
  • The energy of the moving water turning the turbines is converted to electricity.

Advantages

  1. the flow of water is free
  2. very reliable
  3. water can be stored in dams for future use

Disadvantages

  1. dams are expenive to build
  2. can impact wildlife
  3. dams cause upstream flooding
