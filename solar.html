Photovoltaic Cells

Solar Energy

How It Works

  • Solar energy comes from the sun and can be captured with solar panels.
  • Photovoltaic or photoelectric cells convert sunlight directly into electricity.
  • The electricity is used to power lights and provide heat for buildings.

Advantages

  1. free
  2. needs no fuel
  3. produces no waste or pollution

Disadvantages

  1. large power cells are expensive
  2. needs a mostly sunny climate
  3. energy has to be stored
