Wind Turbine Farm

Wind Energy

How It Works

  • Wind is created by the uneven heating of the Earth's atmosphere by the sun.
  • Wind can spin giant wind turbines which converts the energy to mechanical.
  • The mechanical energy is then converted into electricity to be used by humans.

Advantages

  1. free
  2. needs no fuel
  3. produces no waste or pollution

Disadvantages

  1. does not work without wind
  2. can be noisy
  3. may kill birds
