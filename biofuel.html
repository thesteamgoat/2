Biofuels

How It Works

  • Biofuel energy works by using fuels derived from living or dead biological organisms.
  • An example of this is ethanol which is made from corn and mixed with gasoline.
  • Animal wastes such as dung can be used as a fuel and burned for heat.

Advantages

  1. reduces waste materials
  2. tends to be cheap
  3. comes in a variety of forms (both plant and animal)

Disadvantages

  1. creates greenhouse gases when burned
  2. requires time to produce
  3. only a small percentage is used in gasoline
