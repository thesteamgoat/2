Wave/Tidal Turbine

Wave and Tide Energy

How It Works

  • Similar to hydroelectricity, the natural movement of water is captured in turbines.
  • The mechanical energy of waves and tides flows through turbines.
  • Turbines convert the mechanical energy to electricity.

Advantages

  1. produces no waste
  2. needs no fuel
  3. can produce a great deal of energy

Disadvantages

  1. wave energy needs strong and consistent waves
  2. equipment can be noisy
  3. equipment must be able to withstand rough weather
